Hi, my name is Bailey.

I'm an INFJ, designer, photographer, artist, vioinist, and harpist from Cincinnati, Ohio.

I graduated from Miami University in May 2016 with a BA in Interactive Media Studies and minors in Violin Performance and Photography. As a designer, I specialize in UX/UI, web development, and branding and logo design.

I'm a person of many interests who wants to pursue everything I'm passionate about. I design because I love the intersection of creativity and technology. I photograph to express my inner thoughts and view of the world. I make music because it is the way to the soul.

When I'm not doing any of these things I'm probably eating chicken fried rice, listening to hip-hop, or staring longingly out a window at a squirrel.
